About Me

Hello! I'm Miranda Drummond, a tech enthusiast currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence at IE University in Madrid, Spain. My passion for languages, mathematics, and problem-solving drives my ambition and l hope to work in machine learning software engineering and eventually transition into quantitative finance.

My professional strengths encompass approachability, attention to detail, adaptability, and proficiency in multiple languages. These attributes have contributed to my accomplishments, such as winning the 2023 JP Morgan Code for Good Hackathon and serving as Tech Lead & founder at the Google Developers Student Club. I recently completed an internship as a Solutions Architect at Amazon Web Services, where I developed a strong understanding of cloud computing and kernel programming. I am currently working as a research intern at the IMDEA Software Institute, where I am working on incomplete bug fixes in C and C++.

Having gained valuable experience at organizations like JP Morgan Chase & Co. and Healthy.io, I have developed a strong skill set in programming languages and software. Committed to furthering my knowledge and making a meaningful impact in the world of technology, I welcome the opportunity to collaborate on innovative projects. I invite you to explore my portfolio and connect with me to discuss our shared interests or potential collaborations.
